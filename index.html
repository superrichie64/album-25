Who is PulseWave?

PulseWave Records is an independent music company founded in 2025 in Toronto. Built on the belief that music is both culture and community, PulseWave focuses on discovering emerging talent in Citypop-Lofi and Summer-Reggae genres. The company blends grassroots artist development with digital-first promotion strategies to connect local voices to global audiences.
